Data Collection Practices

How we collect and use publicly available web data.

What We Collect

LDVK collects and aggregates publicly accessible data from the web to provide actionable market intelligence reports to our clients. We focus on:

  • Video performance metrics (views, engagement rates, growth trends)
  • Pricing and availability data for e-commerce and travel industry clients
  • Market trends and competitor benchmarking
  • Audience demographics and content performance analytics

Why We Use Residential Proxies

To deliver accurate and comprehensive data to our clients, we use residential proxy services for the following reasons:

  • Geographic coverage: Access geo-restricted content to provide regional market insights for clients operating in multiple countries
  • Scale: Collect data at the volume required for meaningful statistical analysis and trend identification
  • Accuracy: View content as real users see it, without datacenter IP blocking or altered content
  • Rate limits: Distribute requests to avoid overloading source servers while maintaining data freshness

Compliance & Ethics

We only collect publicly available data that any user could access through a web browser.

We respect robots.txt directives and website terms of service.

We do not collect personal data, login-protected content, or any sensitive information.

Our data collection practices comply with GDPR and applicable data protection regulations.

Industries We Serve

We provide data-driven insights for clients in:

  • Media & Entertainment: YouTube analytics, social media trends, content performance
  • E-commerce: Pricing intelligence, product availability, competitor monitoring
  • Travel & Hospitality: Pricing trends, availability monitoring, market intelligence for travel industry clients
  • Advertising: Campaign performance benchmarking, audience insights
